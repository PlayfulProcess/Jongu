Platform Overview

A comprehensive vision for secure attachment, growth, and connection

The Journey of Jongu

In the land of the blind, the one-eyed person is king. But what if instead of kings, we sought companions? What if the journey toward vision was one we undertook together, each offering our unique perspective to illuminate the path?

Jongu exists as a space where authentic human potential can unfold through secure attachment. Built on principles from Marriage and Family Therapy, Nonviolent Communication, and creative expression, we're cultivating a community dedicated to the continuous process of becoming.

Inspired by Jongo, a circular dance and musical tradition brought to Brazil by enslaved Africans, Jongu creates a dynamic interplay between personal expression and community response. The circle maintains the rhythm and energy, while individuals contribute their unique movements and interpretations.

Core Modules

The building blocks of the Jongu experience

1. Jongu Cards

Interactive card experiences for relationship exploration and growth.

  • Multiple play formats: random draw, sequential dialogue, geometric spreads
  • Pre-designed decks across categories including relationship-focused, tarot, trivia and values exploration
  • Session recording for later reflection
  • Asynchronous and synchronous play options

2. Jongu Writing

Collaborative storytelling and reflective writing tools.

  • Evidence-based journaling prompts
  • Creative writing frameworks
  • Flexible sharing options (private, couple, selective)
  • AI-powered assistance (grammar, suggestions, expansion)
  • Publishing and scheduling options

3. Jongu Learning

Step-by-step psychoeducational experiences with interactive elements.

  • Modular learning pathways on relationship topics
  • Interactive elements including reflection prompts and image/video creation
  • Real-world application projects
  • Progress tracking and achievement system
  • Resource library with supplementary materials

4. Jongu Creator

Tools for creating custom content across the platform.

  • Creation tools for custom cards, writing templates, and learning modules
  • AI assistance for content generation
  • Templates and frameworks to simplify creation
  • Publishing options to share with community
  • Analytics on created content usage

5. Jongu Humans

User and professional directory with community features.

  • Badge system based on platform engagement and certifications
  • Professional profiles with services and expertise
  • Resource sharing and following capabilities
  • Community forums and discussions
  • Matching system for mentorship and collaboration

Core Principles

The philosophical foundation of Jongu

1

Joy is the Fuel

Growth flourishes through playful engagement. We believe that learning and development happen most naturally when wrapped in joy and play.

2

Community is the Playfield

Building connections through shared discovery. The collective wisdom of a supportive community enhances individual growth and learning.

3

Everyone is Equal

Users start as "humans" and earn badges through interaction. We believe in an egalitarian approach where credibility is built through contribution.

4

Secure Attachment in a Safe Space

Environment for personal and family growth. We create digital spaces that strengthen attachment security rather than weaken it.

5

Evidence-Informed Play

Research-backed exercises enhanced by AI insights. We combine scientific understanding with technological innovation to create effective experiences.

Technical Requirements

The foundation that supports Jongu's vision

Platform Architecture

  • Frontend: Next.js + React for responsive experiences
  • Backend: Firebase/Supabase for real-time data synchronization
  • AI Integration: OpenAI API for relationship insights
  • Security: End-to-end encryption for sensitive data and privacy controls

Integration Points

  • Single user identity across all modules
  • Consistent AI assistance throughout platform
  • Shared content library accessible to all modules
  • Unified analytics and progress tracking
  • Cross-module recommendations
  • Community features across the whole platform
  • Reviews across the whole platform
